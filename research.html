Research Experience

  2. PhD Thesis

    Oct 2020 — Oct 2023

    SOLEIL synchrotron, Sorbonne University & CEA Saclay

    Soft X-ray radiolysis of water and biomolecules: insights from experiments and simulations.

    • Investigated different timescales of interaction of ionizing radiation with aqueous solutions, using classical and quantum molecular dynamics.
    • Detected and quantified extremely low yields of radiolytic products by developing a time-efficient, highly sensitive in-line analysis setup and microfluidic devices.
    • Used state-of-the-art liquid micro-jet XPS and NAP-XPS for liquid droplets.
    • Lead a team of 5 to perform 10 intensive experimental projects (~1680 h) at the synchrotron facility.
    • Presented work in national/international conferences and to a Jury from USA, France and Prague.
